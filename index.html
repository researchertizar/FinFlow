Welcome to FinFlow
By Researcher Tizar
Your personal money tracker — simple, private, and completely free.

No account needed. Everything stays on your device only.
  • 100% Private — no sign-up, no cloud, no tracking
  • Works Offline — all data saved locally on your phone
  • Super Fast — record a payment in under 5 seconds
    • 💸
    Track Your Money
    There are only 3 things you can record:
  • Spent — money you paid out (groceries, rent, coffee)
  • Received — money coming in (salary, freelance, gift)
  • Transfer — moving money between your own accounts
    • Tap the Add button or the + circle at the bottom anytime.
    🏦
    Add Your Accounts
    An account is simply a place where you keep money.
    Think of it like setting up your wallet sections.
  • Checking — your everyday bank account
  • Savings — money set aside for later
  • Credit Card — money you owe (enter as negative)
  • Cash — physical money in your pocket
    • 🏷️
    Categories = Labels
    Categories help you see where your money goes.
    We've already set up common ones for you!
  • Groceries, Dining — food spending
  • Transport — fuel, bus, taxi
  • Utilities — electricity, water, internet
  • Salary — regular income from work
    • 🎯
    Set Spending Limits
    A budget is a monthly spending limit for a category.
    Like saying "I'll spend max ₹5,000 on food this month."
  • Green bar = you're on track 👍
  • Yellow stripes = getting close to limit ⚠️
  • Red = over budget — time to be careful 🛑
    • ⭐
    Savings Goals
    Set a goal to save for something you want — a vacation, new phone, emergency fund, or anything!

    Track how much you've saved vs your target.
  • Vacation Fund — save ₹30,000 for a trip
  • Emergency Fund — 3-6 months of expenses
  • New Device — save up gradually
    • 🚀
    You're All Set!
    You're all set! Start by adding your accounts, then record your first transaction.

    Tip: go to Settings → Load Demo Data if you'd like to explore with sample numbers first.
  • Home — see your total balance and recent spending
  • Tap + button anytime to record a payment
  • Settings — change currency, theme, export data

    About

    Add Savings

    Savings entries track money you've set aside. Add a savings entry each time you deposit or save an amount.
    ₹

    Add Transaction

    Choose what type of transaction this is, then fill in the amount and details.
    ₹
    10 50 100 500 1,000 5,000

    Add Account

    An account is any place you keep money — bank, wallet, credit card or savings pot. Add one account for each place your money lives.
    ₹

    Add Category

    Categories are labels that help you see where your money goes. Choose a colour and icon so you can spot them instantly in your transaction list.

    Set Budget

    A budget is a monthly spending limit for one category. Example: "I won't spend more than ₹5,000 on Dining Out this month." You'll see a progress bar fill up as you spend.
    ₹
    Roll over unused budget
    Add leftover to next month's limit

    New Goal

    A savings goal is a target you're working toward — holiday, phone, emergency fund. Set the total you need and track how close you get each month.
    ₹
    ₹

    Add Recurring

    Recurring items = regular payments or income. FinFlow alerts you when due, auto-calculates monthly cost, and can create transactions automatically.
